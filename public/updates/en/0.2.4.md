# Update Notice (v0.2.4)
- **Date**: 2026-02-14
- **Description**:
  - **Dynamic Daily Archive Mode**:
    - For late-night workers, you can now choose to keep recording activity under the same "day" until you close the application, even after midnight.
    - Go to Settings > Timetable > 'Daily Archive Mode' and select **Dynamic (Until App Close)**.
  - **Timetable View Improvements**:
    - Sessions extending beyond 24 hours (e.g., 25:00, 26:00) are now visualized correctly on the timeline graph.
  - **Settings UI Improvements**:
    - Moved 'Daily Archive Mode' setting to the **Timetable** tab for better accessibility.
  - **Stability improvements and bug fixes**.
