### AppLayout Code Fragmentation & Refactoring
Refactored the large `AppLayout.tsx` by separating its code into smaller components and hooks based on their roles to maximize maintainability.
- **Component Separation**: Extracted the left activity bar area into `AppSidebarRail.tsx`, and the top title and control area (search, window controls, etc.) into `AppTitleBar.tsx`.
- **Custom Hook Separation**: Extracted the sidebar width resizing logic into `useSidebarResize.ts`, and the auto-completion logic for expired projects into `useExpiredProjectsSync.ts`.
- **Functionality Preservation**: Strictly followed refactoring principles to ensure all original side effects and business logic (searching, sorting, resizing, etc.) operate exactly identical as before.

Date: 2026-03-03 20:55:00

### Further Fragmentation of AppTitleBar & AppSidebarRail
Continuing from the previous refactoring, the internal code within `AppTitleBar.tsx` and `AppSidebarRail.tsx` has been further subdivided to improve maintainability and strictly separate component responsibilities.
- **Notification Popover Separation**: Extracted the hardcoded notification popover code directly positioned inside the `AppSidebarRail.tsx` to its own `NotificationsPopover.tsx` component.
- **Search Bar Separation**: Separated the intricate and state-heavy project search functionalities across mobile and PC viewports into `AppSearchBar.tsx`.
- **Window Controls Separation**: Extracted custom title bar window operations (minimize, toggle maximize, close) to a dedicated `WindowControls.tsx` component.
