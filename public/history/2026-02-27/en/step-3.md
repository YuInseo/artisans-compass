### ✨ Process Name-Based App Recognition
- **Improved Accuracy:** The backend tracking logic has been completely replaced to identify apps solely based on their "Process Name" (e.g., brave, Code) rather than unpredictable window titles.
- **Preventing False Positives:** This prevents incorrect tracking caused by random keywords appearing in web browser tabs or folder names. Simply register the process names (lowercase) in 'Tracked Apps' for reliable tracking.
- **Timetable Display Note:** The reason only 1m 13s (Antigravity project) was shown on your graph is because the **'Work Apps Only'** setting was enabled! Your time is safely being tracked in the background; you just need to add apps like 'Brave' to your 'Work Apps' list or turn off the setting to see all accumulated time.
